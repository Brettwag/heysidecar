Get in touch

Brett Wagner is the founder of Sidecar Design, where he works closely with product teams, founders, and leadership to define direction, establish systems, and ship work that matters. With 15+ years leading design at companies ranging from startups to enterprise, he brings the kind of judgment that only comes from having been in the room.

Sidecar has partnered with organizations across enterprise software, AI products, consumer mobile, design systems, and 0→1 product development — delivering senior design leadership without the overhead of a full-time hire.

Engagements take three forms: fractional design leadership for teams building or scaling a practice; embedded partnership working alongside product managers, engineers, and executives; or focused contract work on a specific product phase or moment of change.

Sidecar is selectively available for new engagements. To start a conversation, reach out at [email protected] or connect on LinkedIn.
